Fashion Inspiration: Vaccination Day

The most important day of your life, after your birth* is your Covid-19 day vaccination.
Whenever you get it it's going to be game-changing ( read the world is going to stop actively trying to kill you), so you might as well dress for it!
Think sleevesless, cardigans, hoodies, t-shirts, you know the stuff that you can take off, the stuff that can always hug you, stuff that will distract you from a needle.
The gallery is filled with outfits to get your vaccine day style game started. Hope you stay safe and get your vaccination asap❤️
