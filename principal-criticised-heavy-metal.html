Petition to Transfer Principal
For Heavy Metal Fashion

Concerned parents in Canada started a petition to remove a highschool principal for wearing Heavy Metal Fashion and promoting “satanic” symbolism. Parents believe that the principal is promoting satanic symbols to kids. Details are summarized in bullet points.

  • Sharon Burns is the principal of Eden High School in St. Catharines, Ontario, and a fan of Iron Maiden and heavy metal. She wears a lot of Iron Maiden and heavy metal inspired Fashion.
  • MS Burns shares her love for heavy metal on her social media accounts. Parents are worried that clothing she wears and music she posts about is promoting satanizm.
  • One parent started a petition on Change.org to have her transferred to a different school. The title is "Eden High School Principal, Sharon Burns, Needs to Be Transferred Immediately!"
  • The pettition is facing a lot of backlash and the parents responded by amending their statement. Now they just claim that they have a problem with a “666” symbol MS Burns shared online.
  • A counter-petition on Change.org, titled “We Need Mrs. Burns” has received more than 12,000 signatures, as it cites the positive impact that the principal has made on the high school.
