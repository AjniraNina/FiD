You ever get a feeling that makes you ​so excited ​that you want to scream it to the world? No, fuck “want”; ​you ​need ​to tell the world.

I like to think that most people will, at least ​once ​in their lives, have a moment like that.

The Degrees of relative importance may vary, as will the actual contents, of course, but what they all have in common is the ability to create an overwhelming sense of “why doesn’t ​everyone ​know about this?”

I have been having that moment for some time now, trying to figure out the best way to tell others about it without literally going from person to person, excitedly shaking them and hoping for a telepathic osmosis…


Then I realized that we already have a device for that; a website.

And, that's how Fashionista in Danger ⚡ was born.

Fashionista in Danger is a website (duuh) for all fashion curious humans out there. It's a concoction of research on psychology of fashion, news and personal fashion stories.

Let me just insert humble brag here;

I've designed, coded and worked on Fashionista in Danger day and night just to make sure that the mission of saving fashionistas in danger ends up being successful. What do I mean by that?

Well, fashionising can be agonizing; in more ways than one. It also has an immense impact on your life. You can use it to change your life or you can get used by it.

If you want to unapologetically utilize it to improve your life you can but just like anything else in life it's an acquired skill.

I want you to personally benefit from this skill and understanding, ability to manipulate your world and yourself using this untapped resource.

Ergo, Fashionista in Danger is a space where together we will dissect clothing and investigate how clothing impacts the world we live in.

To discover why and how fashion truly intenwindes with your experience I warmly recommend a mini fashion festo.

In this article I dig deeper into how clothes can be utilized. And, then you do you. Go wild and read one of the articles on psychology of fashion or have a read on how your daily fashionistas fell for fashion or just get inspired with some fashion galleries.

P.S. Thank you so much for being part of the fashionista in danger experience. Let me know if there is anything you would like to read more of, less of, etc. Can’t to wait to hear from you!
