Fashion Fun: Method Fashion

What happens when method acting meets Fashion? Well, method Fashion of course.

If this is the first time you are hearing about it, well, that's normal because I'm pretty sure I coined the term method Fashion. In a nutshell; artists are priming their audience for their art with Fashion. It's sort of like when actor becomes the character they are suppose to be for the role, all the time. In method Fashion an artist just dresses like the character they are portaying.

To the untrained eye outfits in the gallery might just look like well put together but actually outfits are composed of references to the latest projects.

Method Fashion creates a cohesive illusion and makes art a little bit more real.

Think Zendaya, Lady Gaga and Taylor Swift. With every new project their Fashion changes to support their art.

This approach to promotion is very useful because it helps us, ultra-visual-creatures, connect with whatever the artist is selling.

Method Fashion has been used in past for sure and off red carpets but it is impressive to see it so intentionally used. It's a good reminder that if we want a certain outcome we shouldn't ignore the clothes we wear.
