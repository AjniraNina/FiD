FALLING FOR SHEER TIGHTS

This is a story about how my friend Alan became a cross-dresser. I hope you like it.

I met Alan in Melbourne. In a small complex of apartments next to the ocean. At first glance, Alan is your typical Australian young man. A tradey, loves to party and seems intrinsically considerate.

Alan is so much more than meets the eye.

His apartment floor is filled with disoriented heels, glamorous material is peering from jarred wardrobe doors and a cat is always there to dismiss you. Actually, Alan’s apartment will tell you more about him than he will.

I wondered how did this start, what’was this story his apartment was shouting...

As soon as I got the courage to ask about the shoes on the floor, glam dresses and glitter - his boyish armour dissolved and words just came out.

"My mum was an ordinary woman...

That is until she would get ready to work. Suddenly, she would turn into a little taller, better, more glamorous version of herself. It was jarring and frustrating.

I mean, she never got ready for me like that.
Nonetheless, I wanted her super-power.”

So, he investigated.

He needed to find out how does his mother go from a normie to a Goddess.

The search was exhausting, dead end after dead end. Finally, after many red herrings, he got lucky. Spread out on his mother's bed there they were - sheer tights.

How curious.

He did what every detective does. He reenacted a crime scene. He tried them on.

“Trying them on was like falling down a rabbit hole.”

Imagine, rough skin of a boy, whose hands build castles in the dirt, touching a fragile flimsy fabric for the first time. Must have been magically gentle.

Alan witnesses that it felt like a transient bliss and, it became a poison Ivy of sorts, his home and an escape.

That is that. A superpower was and for ever will be in his hands. There is no way around it. Stockings can change your life. Mystery solved.
