FALLING FOR SHEER TIGHTS

I met Alan in Melbourne. In a small complex of apartments next to the ocean. At first glance, Alan is your typical Australian young man. A tradie, loves to party and seems intrinsically considerate.

Alan is so much more than meets the eye.

His apartment floor is filled with disoriented heels, glamorous material is peering from jarred wardrobe doors and a cat is always there to dismiss you. Actually, Alan’s apartment will tell you more about him than he will, that is until you finally get the privilege to know him.

It’s not much of a secret but nobody ever asks and Alan is not a person who will shove his story at you. I asked.

His boyish armor dissolved and words just came out.

"My mum was an ordinary woman. That is until she would head to work. Suddenly she would turn into a little taller, better, more glamorous version of herself. It was jarring and a bit frustrating. I mean she never got ready for me like that.
Nonetheless, I wanted her super-power”

So, he investigated. After many red herrings Alan finally got lucky. Spread out on his mother's bed there they were, sheer tights. How curious.

He did what every detective does. He reenacted a crime scene.

"Trying them on was like falling down a rabbit hole"

Imagine, rough skin of a boy, whose hands build castles in dirt, touching a fragile flimsy fabric for the first time. Must have been magically gentle.

Alan witnesses that it felt like a transient bliss and, it became a poison Ivy of sorts, his home and an escape.

That is that. His superpower was and forever will be in his hands. There is no way around it. Stockings can supercharge your life. Mystery solved.
