Fashion Inspiration: JoJo's Bizarre Adventure

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hirohiko Araki. The manga series is a bit of a Fashion phenomena that offers a pletphora of inspiration. The series is so iconic that even Gucci colaborated with it.

Think 80’s, Italian Fashion, American Fashion, Pop Art, High Culture, David Bowie, Prince, Fredi Mercury and you’ll find all those references stitched into the outfits of JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure characters. The legend has it that only a very trained eye can cath them all.

So, If you are after something new and fashionable then you must scroll through the gallery.

If there is one thing from Araki's illustrations is that you should never be afraid to reference, experiment and get inspired by the things you love.
