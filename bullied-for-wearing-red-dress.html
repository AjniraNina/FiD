Bullied For Wearing a Dress

A Tennessee student wore a red dress to prom and got bullied for wearing it by the CEO of Visuwell - a telemedicine company. Video of the event went viral and the CEO got fired. As always, details are summarized in bullet points.

  • While the High school senior Dalton Stevens was taking pictures with his boyfriend a middle-aged man, Sam Johnson, came up to him and verbally harassed him for wearing a dress.
  • Johnson called Dalton Stevens an idiot, tried to smack the phone of Steven’s boyfriend Jacob Geittmann, missed and hit Stevens on the back.
  • The video ends with off-camera voices of appears to be adults pleading with a smiling Johnson to stop, with one telling him: "It's just prom. It's kids, it's a bunch of kids. Come on, dude."

    See the video

  • Visuwell responded on Monday by announcing that the company’s board of directors had chosen to terminate Johnson, saying that they unequivocally condemn the behaviour he exhibited. - Yahoo News
