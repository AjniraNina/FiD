Fashion Inspiration: Physical

Sometimes people want to say a lot, they panic, miss the mark and make the story boring.

Apple TV’s show Physical starring Rose Byrne as Sheila Rubin, seems to have a case of wanting to tell a lot.

However, even at risk of being bored, the audience stays, because when writers of Physical do it well, they hold a mirror at self-hate and self-destructiveness of an eating disorder truthfully. Suddenly, all the boredom of a clumsily executed show doesn’t matter.

Same goes for Fashion in the show.

Rose’s character Sheila, wears a lot of simple outfits, her tendency for perfection suffocates her and her outfits reflect it. From time to time, though, she shines in a messy, bright outfit that expertly combines the 70's Fashion with the 80’s Fashion. We can see a glimmer of hope in her outfits. Her willingness to never give up is visible in her outfits.

Sheila’s Fashion offers a realistic kind of style, the one that goes from “meh” to “wow” in a span of 24h. Because you know what; sometimes you just hate your outfit and that’s OK because tomorrow is another day. Anyway, if you are hating your outfit today here is a gallery filled with outfits that might just make your outfit tomorrow shine.
