Swiftian style: Alternative to Swifts official merch

Among the Swifties, there's a shared understanding: the official Taylor Swift merchandise often falls short of expectations.

Taylor Swift's merchandise is often expensive, poorly made and mass-produced.

Luckily, there's a great alternative option: Etsy creators have long been designing unique fan-inspired pieces. Here are a few fan favorites:

I'll try to give these a Y2K fashion magazine vibe, so get ready for some nostalgia!

At the heart of this fab tale is the Etsy boutique TaylorandLucas. They bring a chic fusion of artistry and homage. Each piece of clothing is a stylish story, perfectly weaving you into Swift's enchanting lyrical universe. The craftsmanship is on point, echoing the precision and dedication of haute couture ateliers. It's a whole new level compared to what you'd find in Swift's official store. So, if you're looking to rock your Swiftie pride with major style, this is where it's at.

Equally compelling is the boutique Cornelia Street, offering elegant merch featuring whimsical illustrations, all made-to-order just for you. I’m seriously tempted to get something from their skeleton series.

Lyrical Threads is all about that perfect mix of nostalgic and modern flair, a total must-have in the world of fashion! Take their “Meet Me at Midnight” sweater, for example. It’s got that cool, vintage vibe with just the right amount of contemporary twist. You can feel the journey from maker to wearer in every stitch, capturing the excitement and emotional connection that makes fashion so magical. It’s the ultimate back-to-school piece you won’t want to miss! I really want “ ..it’s a real fucking legacy sweatshirt”.

And then there are the bracelets—in the heart of the DIY Swiftie community, there's a charming and deeply personal trend that's been capturing hearts: handmade Taylor Swift-inspired friendship bracelets.

These bracelets aren't just worn; they're exchanged and treasured, embodying the spirit of friendship that Swift's music celebrates. Rumor has it that's how Travis captured Tay Tay’s heart.

If you are going to the Eras tour, make those friendship bracelets! Trust me.

On my flight back from Sydney, an older lady recognized me as a Swiftie because of my bracelet. We ended up chatting, and I learned all about her life. By the end of our conversation, she had the bracelet.

The point is, there are alternative ways to wear Swift's music on your sleeve. Her official store just doesn't cut it.
