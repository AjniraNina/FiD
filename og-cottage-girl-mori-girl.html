Mori Girl: The OG CottageCore Girly

Well, buckle up, style sleuths and nature lovers alike! Before cottagecore sprinkled its pixie dust across the vast cosmic web that is the Internet, there was another trailblazer fashion philosophy silently budding on the vibrant streets of Japan.

Ah yes, Japan, always one step ahead in the style game, am I right?

Meet the Mori Girl, the 'Forest Girl' in the vernacular, the style icon for those who move with the grace of a forest breeze and daydream like it's a full-time job.

This fashion maven, over a decade ago, did not just breathe life into an intoxicating nostalgia for nature and a laid-back lifestyle, but rather gave it a full-on resuscitation.

Her wardrobe tells a tale of tranquil forests, murmuring streams, and the soft rustle of leaves - a tale subtly whispered through vintage details, botanical prints, and rustic charm.

Reflecting on it now, I realise I've been hoarding a few Mori tales in my closet, mistaking them for cottagecore cameos. This trend, you see, blossomed back in the early 2000s, picking up serious street style cred around 2007-2008. Looking back, I can't help but think my hip art teacher could have been the trend's secret ambassador!

In the orchestra of fashion, the Mori Girl trend dances to the calming rhythm of nature, subtly influencing our style and lifestyle choices, and reminding us to embrace slow living in an ever-speeding world. And isn't that something to be celebrated?

So, dear fashionista in danger, how many Mori tales do you have hanging in your wardrobe? Let's bring the forest girl vibe to life!
