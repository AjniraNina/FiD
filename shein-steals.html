SHEIN Steals From Designers

Hyper fast fashion giant is caught stealing designs from small business, again.

Key Points:

  • A few days ago etsy designer Grace was mildly surprised when she saw her design being sold for only $12 on a SheIn website.
  • Grace was not contacted nor reimbursed for the copy, instead the copy was just sitting there for anyone to but. With no reference to her work.
  • The placement, rough flower pattern, number of flowers, color scheme created by a collaborator @pj.crochet when testing the pattern was coppied. Both of them took to social media to post about the situation.
  • After a swift backlash from social media the design was silently removed from the SheIn website.
  • Shein recently surpassed Amazon as the most installed shopping app in the U.S., and Canada ranks number four when it comes to countries that visit Shein’s website the most. There is no sign that SheIn will stop stealing designs any time soon.
  • You can check out Grace's original desings on her etsy page
