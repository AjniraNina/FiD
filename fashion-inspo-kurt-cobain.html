Fashion Inspiration: Kurt Cobain

"I like to wear dresses because they’re comfortable. Men wearing dresses isn’t controversial.” – Kurt Cobain


Kurt Cobain is not an accidental Fashion icon. He wore clothing that made him happy and comfortable - that’s exactly what every Fashionista strives for.

I mean this man layered, wore dresses, skirts, enjoyed flannels, wool sweaters, ripped jeans, patched jeans, repurposed, wore thrifted clothes and never cared one bit what other people thought of it.

I think we all need that kind of Fashion fearlessness in our lives. Hence, this Fashion Inspiration post ❥!
