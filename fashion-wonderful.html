The Whirlwind of Fashion Wonderful that is Jonathan Van Ness

Hello, fashionistas! Let me tell you about my forever crush, Jonathan Van Ness (JVN). He's a rainbow-riding unicorn in a world of horses, and I'm here for it!

JVN's hair is a fairy tale. It's like Rapunzel met a disco ball and thought, "Let's mix things up!" And his clothes? More colors than a double rainbow and patterns that make a kaleidoscope look boring.

His approach to fashion isn't just a walk, it's a full-on moonwalk on the fashion runway of life. He's not just stepping out in style; he's breakdancing with flair in every outfit.

But it's not just about the hair and the haute couture. His warm approach to life makes me feel like I've been wrapped in a soft cashmere blanket of positivity and acceptance. His projects aren't just projects; they are parties where everyone's invited, and the dress code is 'you do you'.

With every project he does there is a bit of: "You're awesome, and if you forgot, here's a mirror - now remember!"

His style? Impeccable. His energy? Contagious. His heart? As big as his hair. And that, my fashionistas, is saying something.

So here's my toast to JVN, the human glitter bomb of warmth and empowerment. Keep lighting up our lives with your epic style and heart-warming vibes. And remember, in the words of JVN himself, "You're strong, you're a Kelly Clarkson song, you've got this!"
