Message Me!

I’m gonna make one last assumption about you; if you’re here, you probably have something to say.

I read everything you send me, from the messages just saying ‘Hi!’, all the way to the links to articles you think are ripping Fashionista in Danger off, and I appreciate them all. Especially when you send me underreported (or unreported!) stories about issues in the Fashion World.
